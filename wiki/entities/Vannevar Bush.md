# Vannevar Bush

**Type:** person  
**One-line summary:** American engineer and science administrator who envisioned the Memex — a personal knowledge store with associative trails, described in "As We May Think" (1945).

## Overview

Vannevar Bush (1890–1974) directed the U.S. Office of Scientific Research and Development during WWII and is credited with organizing large-scale scientific collaboration. In 1945 he published "As We May Think" in The Atlantic, describing the [[Memex]] — a hypothetical personal information device that anticipated hypertext, the web, and now the LLM Wiki pattern.

## Key Facts

- Published "As We May Think" in The Atlantic, July 1945
- The Memex envisioned associative trails between documents — the value is in the connections, not just the documents
- Could not solve the maintenance problem: who builds and keeps the trails current
- The LLM Wiki pattern answers his maintenance problem
